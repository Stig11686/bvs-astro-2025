## What We Built

I rebuilt their website from scratch with one focus: **make it easy for patients to get in touch**.

### Immediate Visibility

- **Phone number at the top of every page** — visible without scrolling
- **Clear "Book Appointment" buttons** — prominent throughout the site
- **Simple navigation** — key pages visible immediately, everything else in the footer
- **Working contact form** — tested, monitored, and actually delivering enquiries

### Streamlined Reviews

The old process was painful: collect reviews on the website, manually input them, then separately collect Google reviews for SEO. Double the work, double the hassle.

I integrated a Google Reviews widget that automatically pulls and displays their Google reviews on the homepage. **One source, no manual work, better SEO.**

### Easy Content Management

The new WordPress setup is built for their team, not developers:

- Clear, simple editor — no confusing page builders
- Structured content areas — just fill in the blanks
- Blog ready for content marketing — with training on how to write posts that matter

## Key Takeaways

If you run a dental practice (or any healthcare business), your website needs to do three things:

1. **Make contact effortless** — phone number visible, forms that work, clear next steps
2. **Build trust quickly** — reviews, professional design, easy-to-find information
3. **Be maintainable** — your team should be able to update it without calling a developer

Get those right, and your website stops being a cost centre and starts being a patient acquisition tool.
