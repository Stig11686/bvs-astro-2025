## What We Built

I built Rebecca a custom WordPress website designed around two core goals: **easy booking** and **clear pricing**.

### Booking and Pricing Front and Centre

The two things clients want most, accessible in one click:

- **Price list in main navigation** — no hunting, no DMs required
- **Booking button always visible** — multiple pathways to book
- **Treatment pages** — detailed info so clients arrive informed
- **Mobile-first design** — most of her clients browse on phones

### Premium Visual Design

Every element reflects the quality of Rebecca's work:

- **Pinks, golds, and subtle greys** — matching her existing branding
- **Gallery-focused layout** — because in beauty, seeing is believing
- **Signature font styling** — brand consistency throughout
- **Rounded image galleries** — visual softness that suits the industry

### Built for Growth

The site was designed to scale with her business:

- **Easy-to-update CMS** — Rebecca adds gallery images and updates prices herself
- **Local SEO foundations** — targeting "permanent makeup Yorkshire" and related searches
- **Option to auto-generate treatment pages** — when ready to expand content

## Key Takeaways

For beauty businesses, your website needs to do three things brilliantly:

1. **Show the work** — galleries that prove your skill
2. **Make booking effortless** — one click to book or enquire
3. **Display pricing clearly** — don't make clients hunt for it

Get those right, and the website becomes your best salesperson — working 24/7 while you focus on clients.
