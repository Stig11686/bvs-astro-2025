## What We Built

The goal was simple: create a professional website that integrates with their existing property management system and drives direct bookings.

### Direct Booking Integration

The most important feature — seamless booking without third-party fees:

- **PMS integration** — bookings sync directly with their property management system
- **Real-time availability** — guests see what's actually available
- **Secure payment** — handled through trusted payment gateways
- **Instant confirmation** — no waiting, no manual processing

### Professional Presentation

The Coach House has years of reputation. The website needed to reflect that:

- **Clean, professional design** — matching their existing branding
- **Room showcases** — each room presented with photos and amenities
- **Mobile-optimised** — guests researching on phones get the full experience
- **Fast loading** — no one waits for a hotel site

### Easy Management

Hotel staff needed to make quick updates without calling a developer:

- **Simple content updates** — change text and images easily
- **Room availability** — managed through the PMS, reflected on site
- **No technical skills needed** — training provided for the team
