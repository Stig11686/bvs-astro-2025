## Why You Need a Care Plan

**Without ongoing support:**
- WordPress updates break your site (and you don't know how to fix it)
- Security vulnerabilities leave you exposed to hackers
- Your site goes down and you don't notice for days
- Small problems become expensive emergencies
- You waste hours trying to figure things out yourself

**With a care plan:**
- Updates are tested and applied safely
- Security issues are caught and fixed before they cause damage
- Downtime is detected immediately and resolved fast
- Problems are fixed before they escalate
- You have an expert on call when you need help

## What Counts as Support Time?

**Included in your support hours:**
- Fixing things that break
- Making content changes (text, images)
- Adding new pages or sections
- Troubleshooting issues
- Answering questions
- Training you on WordPress

**Not included (quoted separately):**
- New feature development
- Design changes
- Major redesigns
- New integrations

Most clients never use all their included hours - but they're there when you need them.
