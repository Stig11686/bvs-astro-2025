## Why Your Restaurant Needs a Great Website

In 2026, your website is often the first impression customers get of your restaurant. Before they taste your food, they'll judge your online presence. A well-designed website:

- **Drives table bookings** directly, reducing commission fees from third-party platforms
- **Showcases your menu** with mouth-watering imagery and clear pricing
- **Builds trust** with professional design that reflects your venue's atmosphere
- **Improves local SEO** so hungry customers find you on Google

## What Makes a Great Restaurant Website?

After building websites for restaurants and hospitality venues across Yorkshire, we know what works:

- **Speed** – Hungry customers won't wait for slow pages
- **Mobile-first** – 70%+ of restaurant searches happen on phones
- **Stunning photography** – Your food needs to look irresistible
- **Clear calls to action** – Book a table, order online, find us
- **Google integration** – Maps, reviews, and opening hours synced
