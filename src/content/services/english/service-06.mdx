## Common Questions

<Accordion label="Can brides filter dresses by style or designer?" group="faq">

Yes. I can set up filtering by style (A-line, ballgown, etc.), designer, price range, or any other criteria that makes sense for your collection.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Do you work with pre-loved/consignment boutiques?" group="faq">

Absolutely. I understand the unique needs of pre-loved bridal - individual dress pages, stock that changes frequently, and the need to update quickly when items sell.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Can I update the dress gallery myself?" group="faq">

Yes. I'll set up an easy system and train you thoroughly. Adding a new dress takes just a few minutes.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What about appointment booking?" group="faq">

I integrate with popular booking systems or can set up a custom solution. Brides can book 24/7, choose appointment types, and receive automatic confirmations.

</Accordion>

I take time to understand your brand. We'll look at your existing materials, competitors you admire, and discuss the feeling you want to create. I won't start designing until I truly understand your vision.

</Accordion>
