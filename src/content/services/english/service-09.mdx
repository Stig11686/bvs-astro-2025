## Common Questions

<Accordion label="Can you integrate with our Property Management System?" group="faq">

Yes. I work with major PMS providers including Freetobook, Beds24, Little Hotelier, and others. Integration ensures your availability is always accurate.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What about channel management?" group="faq">

Your booking engine can connect to a channel manager to keep OTA availability in sync. This way, direct bookings automatically update your OTA listings.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Do we need professional photography?" group="faq">

High-quality photos make a huge difference. I can recommend hospitality photographers, or work with what you have. Good imagery is worth the investment.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Can guests book specific rooms?" group="faq">

Yes. Room-level booking lets guests choose their preferred room, not just room type. This is especially valuable for properties with unique rooms.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What about seasonal pricing?" group="faq">

Booking systems support dynamic pricing, seasonal rates, minimum stays, and special offers. You control your pricing strategy.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="How do you handle multiple room types?" group="faq">

Each room type gets its own showcase page with gallery, amenities, and pricing. Guests can browse and compare before booking.

</Accordion>
