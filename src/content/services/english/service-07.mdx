## The Problem With Most Dental Websites

Let's be honest: most dental practice websites were built years ago and it shows.

**Common issues I see:**
- Outdated design that doesn't reflect your modern surgery
- Phone number buried or impossible to find
- Not mobile-friendly (patients search on phones)
- No online booking option
- Slow loading times
- Generic templates that look like every other dentist

Your website should be your best receptionist - professional, helpful, and always available.
