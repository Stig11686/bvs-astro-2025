## What Makes a Great Salon Website?

After building websites for salons and beauty clinics, we know what attracts and converts clients:

- **Elegant design** – Clean, sophisticated aesthetics that reflect your brand
- **Mobile-first** – Most beauty searches happen on phones
- **Fast loading** – Nobody waits for slow pages
- **Easy booking** – Minimum clicks from landing to appointment
- **Social proof** – Reviews, testimonials, and Instagram integration
- **Clear pricing** – Transparency builds trust
