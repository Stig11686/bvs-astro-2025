## Real Example: What an Audit Uncovered

**The Problem:** A dental practice couldn't understand why their website wasn't generating enquiries despite decent traffic.

**What the Audit Found:**
- Phone number only visible after scrolling
- Contact form was broken (enquiries going nowhere)
- Mobile menu was confusing
- No clear call-to-action on any page
- Google Reviews hidden at the bottom

**The Result After Fixing:**
The practice now receives **30+ enquiries per month** - issues they'd lived with for years were fixed in a few weeks.

## Who This Is For

A website audit is perfect if you:

- **Aren't getting enquiries** - You have traffic but no leads
- **Don't know what's wrong** - Something's off but you can't pinpoint it
- **Built it yourself** - DIY site that might have issues you're not aware of
- **Haven't updated in years** - Your site has been neglected
- **Want an expert opinion** - Before investing in a redesign

This is **not** for brand new websites or sites that are clearly working well. If you're getting consistent leads and your site loads fast, you probably don't need an audit.
