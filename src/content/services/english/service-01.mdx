## How It Works

Working with me is straightforward:

**1. Discovery Call (Free)**
We'll talk about your business, your goals, and what's not working with your current website. No pressure, no jargon - just an honest conversation about whether I can help.

**2. Proposal & Planning**
If we're a good fit, I'll send you a clear proposal with fixed pricing. No surprises. Once approved, we'll map out your site structure and content needs.

**3. Design & Build**
I'll design and build your site, keeping you involved at every stage. You'll see progress on a staging site and can give feedback as we go.

**4. Launch & Training**
Once you're happy, we launch. I'll train you on how to update your site yourself, so you're never dependent on me for simple changes.

**5. Ongoing Support**
Optional care plans keep your site secure, updated, and performing. Or just call me when you need changes - I'm always here.

## Common Questions

<Accordion label="How much does a website cost?" group="faq">

Most of my WordPress websites range from **£1,500 to £5,000** depending on complexity. Simple brochure sites are at the lower end; e-commerce or sites with custom functionality are higher. I'll give you a fixed quote upfront - no hourly billing surprises.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="How long does it take?" group="faq">

Typically **4-8 weeks** from kickoff to launch. The biggest variable is how quickly you can provide content (text, images, etc.). I can help with content if needed.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Do I need to know anything technical?" group="faq">

Not at all. I handle all the technical stuff. After launch, I'll train you on making basic updates yourself - it's easier than you think.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What about hosting and ongoing costs?" group="faq">

I offer UK-based hosting from **£25/month** which includes security updates, backups, and support. You're not locked in - you can host elsewhere if you prefer.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Can you help with an existing WordPress site?" group="faq">

Yes. Whether you need a redesign, performance improvements, or just ongoing maintenance, I can help with existing sites too.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What if I'm not happy with the design?" group="faq">

You'll see the design before any code is written and can request changes at every stage. I don't consider a project finished until you're genuinely happy with it.

</Accordion>
