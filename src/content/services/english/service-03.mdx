## SEO-Friendly Web Development

Our approach to SEO focuses on the areas where web development makes the biggest difference:

- **Technical SEO Setup** – clean code, schema markup, and optimized site architecture.
- **Performance Optimization** – fast loading speeds that improve rankings and user experience.
- **Mobile-Friendly Design** – responsive builds that meet Google’s mobile-first standards.
- **On-Page SEO Basics** – page titles, headings, and meta descriptions done right.
- **Analytics & Tracking** – integration with Google Analytics and Search Console.
