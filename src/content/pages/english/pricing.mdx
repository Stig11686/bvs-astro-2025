## Website Builds

All builds include: custom WordPress design, mobile-responsive layout, on-page SEO foundations, contact form setup, Google Analytics 4 + Search Console installation, and a handover training session.

**A note on pages:** Page counts refer to individually designed pages (homepage, about, services, contact, etc.). Content templates — blog, shop, portfolio — are included as standard and don't count toward your page total. Migrated content is priced separately (see below).

---

## Common Questions

<Accordion label="Do you charge by the hour?" group="faq">

No. All projects are quoted at a fixed price upfront. You'll always know exactly what you're paying before work starts.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What if my project doesn't fit neatly into a package?" group="faq">

Get in touch and describe what you need. I'll put together a bespoke quote. The packages above cover the majority of projects — anything outside them gets priced individually.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Can I add a monthly plan to a site you didn't build?" group="faq">

Yes, for existing WordPress sites. I'll do a quick audit first to make sure everything is in good shape, then we set up the plan from there.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Are there any other ongoing costs?" group="faq">

Your monthly plan covers hosting. If you use third-party tools (booking systems, email marketing platforms, etc.) those are billed directly by the provider — I'll always flag these during the project.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Do you offer payment plans for builds?" group="faq">

Yes. Builds are typically split 50% upfront, 50% on launch. For larger projects I can arrange a three-stage payment schedule — just ask.

</Accordion>
