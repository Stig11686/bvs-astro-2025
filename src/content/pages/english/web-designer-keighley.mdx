## Keighley Client Success Story

### Antler Interiors

<OptimizedImage
  src="/images/portfolio/antler-interiors.png"
  alt="Antler Interiors website"
  class="my-6 rounded-lg"
/>

Antler Interiors is a premium joinery and interior design company serving clients across West Yorkshire. Rob and Aaron needed a website that matched the quality of their craftsmanship and helped them attract higher-value projects from both private clients and industry professionals.

**The Challenge:** Create a site that works for two audiences — homeowners looking for bespoke joinery, and architects/surveyors checking credentials before awarding contracts.

**What We Built:**

- Image-led design that lets their craftsmanship do the talking
- Custom project showcase with galleries for different work types
- Easy-to-update CMS — they just add content, the design handles itself
- Clear calls-to-action for consultations and portfolio browsing
- WooCommerce integration for selling products online (added later)

**The Result:** A professional site that showcases their portfolio, builds credibility with both audiences, and generates regular enquiries for high-value projects.
