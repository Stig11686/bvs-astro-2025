## York Client Success Story

### Maidens and Ravens Bridal Boutique

<OptimizedImage
  src="/images/portfolio/maidens-and-ravens.png"
  alt="Maidens and Ravens bridal boutique website"
  class="rounded-lg my-6"
/>

Maidens and Ravens is a bridal boutique with a difference — celebrating individuality rather than conforming to stereotypical bridal imagery. Elizabeth, the owner, wanted a website as distinctive as her offering, with gothic and mystic themes woven throughout.

**The Challenge:** Create an online experience that captures the magic of finding "the one" dress while reflecting the boutique's unique personality.

**What We Built:**
- Striking menu design with background imagery — something you don't see on most websites
- WooCommerce catalogue to showcase one-of-a-kind dresses (without online checkout — fitting is essential)
- Fixed "Book Appointment" button always visible on screen
- Individual enquiry options for specific dresses
- Transition animations that add wow factor

**The Result:** A website brides love to browse, with **30 Google reviews in the first three months** and **1,000+ visitors per month**. Brides regularly book appointments saying "I found you through your website."
