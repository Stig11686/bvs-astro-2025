## Common Questions

<Accordion label="How much does a website cost in Skipton?" group="faq">

Most Skipton businesses I work with invest between £1,500 and £3,500 for a professional WordPress website. The range depends on complexity — a clean brochure site sits at the lower end, while e-commerce or sites with booking systems, catalogues or custom features will be more. I'll give you a clear, fixed-price quote after our first conversation. No vague estimates, no surprises.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="How long does it take to build a website?" group="faq">

Most projects launch within 4–6 weeks from our kick-off call. The biggest factor is usually how quickly content and feedback comes back — the faster you can review and respond, the faster we move. If you need something sooner, ask me about my Websites in 7 Days service.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Do I need to provide the content?" group="faq">

You know your business better than anyone, so your input is essential — but I'll guide you through exactly what's needed and help you shape it. If writing isn't your thing, I can help with copy as part of the project. Most clients find it less painful than they expected.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="Will I be able to update the website myself?" group="faq">

Yes. I build on WordPress specifically because it's straightforward to manage. After launch I'll train you on adding blog posts, updating text, swapping images and anything else you'll need day-to-day. Most clients are comfortable making basic updates within an hour of training.

</Accordion>

<Accordion label="What about hosting and ongoing costs?" group="faq">

I offer managed WordPress hosting from £25/month, which covers hosting, security, backups and updates — everything looked after so you don't have to think about it. Your domain name is typically £10–15/year on top. If you'd prefer to host elsewhere, I'll help you get set up — no obligation to use my hosting.

</Accordion>
