## Ilkley Client Success Story

### Ilkley Dental Care

<OptimizedImage
  src="/images/portfolio/ilkley-dental-care.png"
  alt="Ilkley Dental Care website"
  class="my-6 rounded-lg"
/>

Ilkley Dental Care is a well-established practice that needed a modern website to match their quality of patient care. Their old site had a hidden menu, difficult navigation, and a broken contact form they didn't even know about.

**The Problems:**

- Contact form wasn't working (they were losing enquiries)
- Menu only appeared after scrolling — confusing for visitors
- Phone number buried, no clear call-to-action
- Staff found it hard to update, so it was often out of date
- Google Reviews were being collected twice with manual input

**What We Fixed:**

- Rebuilt the contact form (obviously)
- Phone number prominent at the top of every page
- Clear "Book Appointment" buttons throughout
- Simplified navigation — key pages visible immediately
- Automated Google Reviews widget — no more manual work
- Easy-to-update WordPress CMS for staff

**The Result:** Now generating **30+ enquiries per month** from the same traffic. The team actually believes in their website now.
